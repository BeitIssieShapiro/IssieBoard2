Open question: Action key colors

v1 had 4 separate colors (space, delete, enter, other). v2 style groups apply one bgColor to all members. Two options:

  • Option 1: Single "Action Keys" group, use the most prominent color (space). Simpler but loses per-key granularity.
  • Option 2: Create 4 separate groups ("Space Key", "Delete Key", "Enter Key", "Other Keys"). Preserves full fidelity.
