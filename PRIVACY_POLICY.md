# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 10, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to IssieBoard and IssieVoice (collectively, "the Apps"), developed by Issie Shapiro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our Apps handle information.

## Information We Collect

### IssieBoard (Keyboard Extension)

IssieBoard is a custom keyboard application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- **Typed text or keystrokes** - Nothing you type is collected, stored, or transmitted to us or any third party
- **Personal information** - No names, email addresses, or contact information
- **Usage data** - No analytics or usage statistics
- **Device information** - No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data

### IssieVoice (Communication App)

IssieVoice is an assistive communication application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- **Typed messages or spoken text** - All text and voice output remains on your device
- **Saved sentences** - Stored locally on your device only
- **Personal information** - No names, email addresses, or contact information
- **Usage data** - No analytics or usage statistics
- **Device information** - No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data

## Data Storage

Both Apps store configuration data and user preferences locally on your device:

- **Keyboard configurations** - Layout preferences, key settings, and customizations
- **Saved sentences** (IssieVoice only) - Frequently used phrases stored locally
- **App settings** - Voice preferences, language settings, and display options

All data is stored using your device's standard storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS) and remains on your device. This data is not backed up to cloud services and is not accessible to us.

## Network Access

The Apps do **not** require or use internet connectivity. They do not:

- Send data to remote servers
- Download content from the internet
- Include analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements
- Communicate with external services

## Third-Party Services

The Apps do not integrate with, use, or share data with any third-party services, including:

- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- Social media platforms

## Permissions

The Apps request only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

### IssieBoard
- **No special permissions required** - Standard keyboard extension permissions only

### IssieVoice
- **Text-to-Speech** - Used to speak typed text aloud (processed entirely on-device)
- **Storage** - Used to save and load frequently used sentences locally

The Apps do not request access to:
- Camera or photos
- Microphone
- Contacts
- Location
- Network communication (beyond what the system requires)

## Children's Privacy

The Apps are designed for users of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information or data from our users, the Apps comply with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

## Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, there is no transmission of data that could be intercepted. All settings and saved content remain securely stored using your device's standard security mechanisms.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **To delete IssieBoard data:** Uninstall the keyboard extension from your device settings
- **To delete IssieVoice data:** Uninstall the app from your device, or clear app data in your device settings

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect, store, or process any personal information:

- There is no data to access, correct, or delete from our servers
- There is no data to export or transfer
- There are no marketing communications to opt out of

All user data remains under your complete control on your device.
